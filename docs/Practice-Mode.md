# Practice Mode

Practice Mode simulates the real ham radio exam experience.

## How It Works

1. Select your license class (Technician, General, or Extra)
2. Answer the questions like you would in the real exam
3. Review your results at the end

## Features

### Real Exam Simulation
- No instant feedback during the exam
- Questions are shuffled each time
- You must answer each question to proceed

### Question Counts
- **Technician:** 35 questions
- **General:** 35 questions
- **Extra:** 50 questions

### Passing Score
You need a score of **74% or higher** to pass (same as the real exam).

### Quick Exam
In Settings, enable "Quick Exam" to:
- Automatically advance to the next question after answering
- Speed up your practice sessions

## Navigation

- Use **Previous** to go back to a previous question (if not in Quick Exam mode)
- Use **Next Question** to move forward
- Click **Finish** when you're done
- Use **Exit Exam** to leave the practice session early

## Results

After completing the practice exam, you'll see:
- Your score percentage
- Number of correct/wrong answers
- Pass/Fail status
- Option to view incorrect answers
- Option to restart or go back home

## Tips

- Treat it like the real exam - don't peek at answers!
- Use Quick Exam mode to practice speed
- Review your incorrect answers to identify weak areas
- Track your progress over time
